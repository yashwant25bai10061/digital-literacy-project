Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Never use a UPI PIN to get money, use it to send.
- Before scanning of any QR code, make sure to check the name of the receiver.
- Disregard Digital Arrest warnings by people who call in claiming to be police.
- Turn on 2 step authentication on WhatsApp and Instagram now.
- Strong and unique passwords should be used and not something easy like birthday.
- Do not tap the links to WhatsApp: Free Recharge and part-time Jobs.
- Do not give your OTP to anybody even your bank.
- One should not use a public Wi-Fi to do banking or any other sensitive logins.
