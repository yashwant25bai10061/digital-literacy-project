Social Media Checklist

Do's:

- Make your profiles private in order to manage your audience.
- Connect with LinkedIn workers to establish your career.
- Share your success stories so as to showcase your diligence.
- Postthink, to eliminate future digital regrets.
- Photos were tagged during audits to eliminate anything that may have embarrassed the person.

Don'ts:

- No illegal acts, underage drinking pictures.
- Public feeds Do not praise or denounce professors or internships.
- Do not broadcast your position.
- Avoid the use of inappropriate words that recruiters can detect.
- Do not share too much of your personal details such as student ID.
